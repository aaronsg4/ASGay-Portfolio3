About me.

Hi, I'm Aaron Gay. I'm a developer, an entrepreneur, and a problem-solver. I have a strong sense of detail that I apply thoughtfully throughout the creative process.

I love to push myself into unfamiliar territory, whether that happens to be a new city or a new concept - I believe new and unique experiences are the spice of life. Thinking outside of the box and coming up with a fresh perspective on things is what I do best. I'm always up for a challenge!

I've worked as an analyst crunching numbers, and as a business owner working with an extensive network of clients. Through web development, I'm able to capitalize on my analytical and problem solving skills while continuing to engage with people.

Other passions include horticulture, the great outdoors, the stock market, and travel anywhere. Let's build something together!
